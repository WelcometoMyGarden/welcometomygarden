# Translation guidelines

## General

### Tone of voice

- **Warm and personal.** Generally, the platform speaks directly to users in the second person (see language-specific exceptions below).
- **Community-minded.** WTMG is a citizen initiative; the copy reflects solidarity and a shared spirit.
- **Light and encouraging.** Rulesand instructions are framed positively.

### The following words should never be translated

- WTMG
- Welcome To My Garden
- Superfan
- Slowby

## Glossary

This table contains agreed-upon translations for specific words:

| English (en) | Dutch (nl)   | French (fr) | German (de)      | Spanish (es)      |
| ------------ | ------------ | ----------- | ---------------- | ----------------- |
| slow travel  | traag reizen | voyage lent | langsames Reisen | los viajes lentos |
| host         | host         | hôte        | Gastgeber        | anfitrión         |
| member       | lid          | membre      | Mitglied         | miembro           |
| membership   | lidmaatschap | adhésion    | Mitgliedschaft   | membresía         |

Generally, derived or related forms of these words should also be translated with their equivalent form in other languages, except if explicitly pinned in the dictionary. E.g.: slow traveller (en) becomes trage reiziger (nl), but membership becomes adhésion (fr).

## Per-language guidelines

### Dutch (nl)

- **Informality**: In Dutch, we use the informal _je/jij_ (never _u_).

### French (fr)

- **Respectful language**: In French, using `vous` instead of `tu`
- **Inclusive language**: The French translation uses inclusive writing with mid-dot notation: _voyageur·se·s_, _citoyen·nes_, _passionné·es_. Apply the inclusive-writing convention appropriate to the target language if one exists.
